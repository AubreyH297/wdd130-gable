Famous Cyberattacks in History

Cyberattacks have changed the world in significant ways. Some attacks have disrupted businesses, governments, and critical infrastructure.

Attacks That Changed Cybersecurity

Examples such as WannaCry, Stuxnet, and the Colonial Pipeline attack demonstrate the importance of strong cybersecurity practices.

Studying previous attacks helps cybersecurity professionals understand threats and better prepare for future incidents.

WannaCry

WannaCry was a ransomware attack that spread rapidly across computer systems around the world. It locked important files and demanded payment from victims.

Stuxnet

Stuxnet was a highly advanced computer worm created to target industrial control systems. It demonstrated how cyberattacks could affect physical equipment.

Colonial Pipeline

The Colonial Pipeline attack disrupted fuel distribution and showed how ransomware can affect critical infrastructure and everyday life.

Why Study Cyberattacks?

Learning about past cyberattacks helps organizations recognize weaknesses, improve security systems, train employees, and respond more effectively when threats occur.
