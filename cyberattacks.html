Famous Cyberattacks in History

Cyberattacks have changed the world in significant ways. Some attacks have disrupted businesses, governments, and critical infrastructure.

Examples such as WannaCry, Stuxnet, and the Colonial Pipeline attack demonstrate the importance of strong cybersecurity practices.

Cyberattacks Around the World

Studying previous attacks helps cybersecurity professionals understand threats and better prepare for future incidents.

WannaCry

WannaCry was a ransomware attack that spread rapidly across computer systems throughout the world. It locked files and demanded payment from victims.

Stuxnet

Stuxnet was an advanced computer worm designed to target industrial systems. It showed that cyberattacks can damage physical equipment as well as digital information.

Colonial Pipeline

The Colonial Pipeline ransomware attack disrupted fuel distribution and demonstrated how cybercrime can affect critical infrastructure and everyday life.

Why Study Past Cyberattacks?

Learning about past cyberattacks helps organizations identify weaknesses, strengthen security systems, train employees, and respond more effectively to future threats.
