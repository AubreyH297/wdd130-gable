How Digital Investigations Work

Digital forensics investigators gather and analyze evidence from computers, networks, and mobile devices to determine how cyber incidents occurred.

Investigators use specialized tools to recover deleted files, examine logs, and identify malicious activity.

Digital investigations are essential for identifying attackers, supporting law enforcement, and improving cybersecurity defenses.
